Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find answers to the most common questions about ArtEvo, auctions, artists, and purchases.

1. What is ArtEvo?
ArtEvo is an online platform designed to promote and support local artists by showcasing their evolving artworks across different art forms. It also provides a safe and transparent auction-style system for buying and selling art.
2. How does the auction system work?
Users can browse available artworks, place bids within the specified time, and the highest bidder wins once the auction ends. All bidding information is displayed clearly to ensure transparency.
3. How can artists join ArtEvo?
Artists may apply through our Artist Registration Form. Once approved, they can create a profile, upload artworks, and participate in auctions.
4. What types of art can be showcased?
ArtEvo accepts a wide range of art forms including: Digital art, Traditional paintings, Watercolor, Mixed media, Ceramics & sculpture, Photography, Graphic design, Concept art and more. As long as the work is original and created by the artist.
5. Is there a fee for artists to join?
ArtEvo does not charge membership fees. However, a small service fee may be applied to artworks successfully sold through the platform.
6. How do buyers pay for artworks?
Payments are made through secure payment options such as GCash, credit/debit card, or bank transfer. Details will be provided once a bid is won.
7. How are artworks delivered?
Artists are responsible for packaging their artworks carefully. Buyers may choose delivery via courier, pickup arrangements, or digital file delivery (for digital artwork).
8. Can I return a purchased artwork?
Returns are generally not allowed unless the item arrives damaged or does not match the description. Concerns must be reported within 48 hours upon receiving the artwork.
9. How do I know the artwork is authentic?
Every artist is verified through a review process, and artworks listed include descriptions, signatures, certificates (if applicable), and high-quality photos.
10. Who do I contact for support?
You may reach us through: Email: support@artevo.com, Messenger / Social Media links in the footer. We are available Monday–Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.
